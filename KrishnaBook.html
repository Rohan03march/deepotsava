Read Krishna Book

The book “Krishna - The Supreme Personality of Godhead”,
written by Srila Prabhupada (Founder-Acharya of ISKCON),
summarises the 10th Canto of the Srimad Bhagavatam.

It narrates Lord Krishna's divine pastimes , which He performed on this earth around 5,000 years ago .
Regularly reading these pastimes fosters devotion in our hearts and keeps us connected with the Lord.

Read any chapter of the book, particularly those describing the pastimes of Damodara-lila, Govardhana-lila, or the killing of the demon Narakasura , which are relevant to the holy month of Kartika.
