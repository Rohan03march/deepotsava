Offer a Ghee Lamp to Domadara

Offering a ghee lamp to Lord Damodara is a vital part of the Deepotsava celebrations during the sacred month of Kartika. This act of devotion attracts immense blessings from the Lord and brings abundant spiritual rewards, as described in the scriptures.

Offer a ghee lamp with devotion to the Deity or to a picture of Lord Krishna, Vishnu, or any of His incarnations, such as Lord Rama or Narasimha .
You may also recite the Damodarashtakam while offering the lamp.
