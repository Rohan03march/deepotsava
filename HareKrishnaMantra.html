Chant the Hare Krishna Maha mantra

Chanting and hearing the Hare Krishna Mahamantra is recommended in the scriptures as the most sublime and effective method of spiritual advancement in the current age of Kali-yuga.
As you practice this daily, you will experience inner peace, happiness, and positivity.

Chant 108 times, preferably with a japa mala, while listening to this recording of
Srila Prabhupada (Founder-Acharya of ISKCON) chanting the Hare Krishna Mahamantra.
