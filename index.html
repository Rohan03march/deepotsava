About Deepotsava!

A Festival of Lights
and Joy

Deepotsava, often referred to as the Festival of Lights, is a vibrant celebration that symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and knowledge over ignorance. This festival, celebrated primarily in various parts of India, brings communities together in a joyful atmosphere filled with festivities.

During Deepotsava, homes and public spaces are beautifully decorated with diyas (oil lamps) and colorful rangolis. The flickering lights create a magical ambiance, inviting families and friends to partake in the celebrations. Traditional music, dance performances, and feasts featuring delicious sweets and snacks add to the joyous spirit.
